About

My name is Victor Guillen and I'm the owner of this Astrophography website.

I started with this hobby around 5 years ago, when I was looking at stunning pictures from deep space taken by NASA's Hubble Telescope and many others astrophotographers and amateur astronomers around the world. This is a very exciting hobby that can easily move you from success to frustration and back to success. It requires patience and perseverance.

I work as Software developer for a technology company, with many years of experience developing a diverse pool of applications and databases. One of my interests it is Cybersecurity due to the high demand it has in our technology-bound lives.

I am also a watch collector; I love watches regardless of their price, brand and complications, I just love them and that's another of my hobbies.

Thanks for visiting my website, and I hope its content is useful to you.

Victor Guillen
