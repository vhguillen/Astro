Astrophotography

This website is devoted to the exciting and amazing hobbie of Astrophotography. Have you ever wonder how to get great pictures of objects in the nightsky? Do you have a telescope and want to take pictures of the moon, planets or stars?

You will find the necessary introductory guidelines on how to achieve and take pictures from celestial objects.

This hobby requires patience and perseverance; there will be nights where you can spend hours and not get anything and that's frustrating! there will be some other nights where you can get amazing images in a few hours, and that's why this hobby requires perseverance and work on the technique until you achieve the desired results.

Moon close up - Click on the image to visit the photo Gallery

There are many websites about Astrophotography, but most of them are highly technical and most of the time discourage the beginners due to the complexity inherent to this hobby. I have outlined the basic procedures that a beginner can utilize to start with this amazing hobby.

Welcome to my website and enjoy the content, I hope this can be of good use for you, but remember: always experiment and use your criteria and best judgement. These are just guidelines to make your life easier in this great hobby.
